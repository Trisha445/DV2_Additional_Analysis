Methodology Notes

These visualizations combine data from multiple ABS sources and apply various analytical techniques:

  • Economic Resilience Score: Composite index incorporating employment stability, economic diversity, and growth indicators
  • Skills Mismatch Index: Calculated using the differential between job vacancy rates and qualified workforce availability
  • Intervention Priority: Determined through multi-factor analysis of employment rates, skills gaps, and regional characteristics
